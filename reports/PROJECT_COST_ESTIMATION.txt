### Infrastructure Cost Optimization

#### Cost-Saving Strategies

1. **Reserved Instances** (AWS/GCP)
   - 1-year commitment: 20% savings
   - 3-year commitment: 40% savings
   - **Potential Savings**: $15,000/year

2. **Spot Instances** for Non-Critical Workloads
   - 50-70% cost reduction
   - **Potential Savings**: $8,000/year

3. **Database Optimization**
   - Read replicas for scaling
   - Connection pooling
   - **Potential Savings**: $5,000/year

4. **CDN Optimization**
   - Cache optimization
   - Compression
   - **Potential Savings**: $2,000/year

**Total Potential Infrastructure Savings: $30,000/year**
